Pumpkin Turkey Chili

Description:

A delicous dish with turkey and pumpkin that is easy and quick to make

Ingredients:

    1 tablespoon vegetable oil 1 cup chopped onion ½ cup chopped green bell pepper ½ cup chopped yellow bell pepper 1 clove garlic, minced 1 pound ground turkey 1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes 2 cups pumpkin puree 1 ½ tablespoons chili powder or more to taste ½ teaspoon ground black pepper 1 dash salt ½ cup shredded Cheddar cheese ½ cup sour cream
